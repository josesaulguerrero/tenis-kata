## Scoring system
    - 0 points -> love.
    - 1 point -> fifteen.
    - 2 points -> thirty.
    - 3 points -> forty.
    - both players are on zero -> love-all.
    - both players are on one -> fifteen-all.
    - both players are on two -> thirty-all.
    - both players are on three -> deuce.

## Rules
    - if both players are over three, then find out who is leading.
    - a player wins when their score equals or is over their opponent's score plus two.

## Methods
    - wonPoint(): void -> sums up a point to the player score.
    - getScore(): String -> returns a string with the calculated score.
